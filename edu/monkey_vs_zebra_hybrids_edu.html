Monkey vs Zebra — Hybrids & OP Cool-but-Dumb

Hybrid spawn rules, species, sample hybrids, and a tiny demo. Hybrid chance is low — save the chaos for rare moments.

Hybrid rules (one line)

When two species meet, there's a small chance (configurable) to spawn a hybrid that combines one major trait from each parent plus one random quirky trait.

New species (short)

  • Firefox — speed burst — glowing ember trail reveals hidden enemies
  • Glacowl — aerial glide — freezes puddles into slippery traps
  • Thornox — kamikaze charge — spikes damage nearby on impact
  • Shellaroo — bounce shield — stores one hit and ricochets attackers
  • Bloomrat — swarm summoner — summons pollen motes that heal allies
  • Voltpuppy — short-range stun — chain-stuns weak enemies
  • Quillbat — ranged barbs — fires ricocheting quill projectiles
  • Luminipig — area buff — glowing aura increases crit at night
  • Mossquid — camouflage blob — hides on green terrain
  • Boulderbeetle — armor soak — regenerates armor when stationary
  • Miragedeer — decoy dash — fading afterimage draws aggro
  • Searhawk — dive scorch — creates temporary lava patches
  • Frostpanda — slow field — cold cloud that slows enemies
  • Nectarcat — charm pounce — charms enemies to follow briefly
  • Heliohare — solar leap — gains power in daylight

Example hybrids (short)

Emberape — (Monkey + Firefox): nimble climber, flaming fists, ignites branches for wall-run speed.
Froststriped — (Zebra + Glacowl): airborne zebra, chilly hoofprints slow pursuers.
Thimblemonk — (Monkey + Thornox): vine-covered primate that throws thorn bombs and clings to walls.
Pollinazon — (Zebra + Bloomrat): sprinting grazers that sprout healing pollen clouds.

Hybrid spawn rate suggestion: 1–5%; cap hybrids per match to 1–3. Give big abilities long cooldowns and goofy AI quirks.
